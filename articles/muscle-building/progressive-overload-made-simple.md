Whether a beginner or an advanced lifter, every effective strength program is built on one principle: **progressive overload** — consistently increasing the demands placed on muscles over time.

## Why muscles grow

Muscle hypertrophy is an adaptive response to stress. When a muscle is challenged beyond what it's used to, it repairs itself slightly stronger and larger to handle future demands. Without progressively increasing that challenge, adaptation stops.

## Five ways to progress

Weight isn't the only variable. Progress can come from:
1. **More weight** at the same reps
2. **More reps** at the same weight
3. **More sets** in the same session
4. **Less rest** between sets
5. **Better range of motion** or control
